About CampusVoice

Making student voices
heard and acted upon

CampusVoice is an anonymous feedback platform built for colleges and universities. We believe meaningful change starts with honest, structured student input — and that administration needs the right tools to listen.

🔒

100% Anonymous

No login required. No personal data collected. Your identity is never linked to your feedback — ever.

🤖

AI-Powered Insights

Built on Claude AI, every submission gets real-time sentiment analysis and admin reports are generated intelligently from patterns across all feedback.

📊

Structured Feedback

Department tagging, category selection, and star ratings make it easy to filter, compare, and act on specific areas of campus life.

🎯

Action-Oriented

Every insight is designed to surface something addressable — not just sentiment scores, but specific recommendations administrators can act on.

Process
How It Works
1

Write Your Feedback

Select your department and category, rate your experience, and describe it in your own words.

2

AI Analyses It

Claude instantly analyses sentiment and key themes. No human reads your raw feedback without permission.

3

Insights Surface

Patterns emerge in the Insights dashboard, giving administration clear, data-backed priorities to address.
